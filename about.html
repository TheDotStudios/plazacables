About Plaza

Powering India with Trusted Electrical Solutions

Over five decades of innovation, quality, and reliability in wires, cables, and electrical infrastructure.

Founded in 1975, Plaza Group has grown into a trusted name in India’s electrical industry. Through our brands - Plaza Wires, PCG, and Action Wires - we provide reliable and high-performance electrical products designed for homes, industries, and large infrastructure projects. Our journey is driven by innovation, uncompromising quality, and a deep commitment to safety and sustainability. With decades of expertise and a forward-looking vision, we continue to build solutions that power progress across the nation.

Our Journey

Five decades of growth, innovation, and building India's electrical future — one milestone at a time.

1975

Establishment of RK Cables, a family enterprise engaged in marketing domestic wires and cables.

1991
Brand Launch

RK Cables converted and registered as Plaza Cables brand and introduced to the market for providing all electrical solutions.

1995
Manufacturing

The first manufacturing plant is set up in Baddi, Himachal Pradesh to manufacture house wiring under the Plaza brand.

2000

Another chain of Plaza Electrical Industries — a partnership dissolved and converted to incorporate Navratna Wires Private Limited under the umbrella of Plaza Cables.

2005

Navratna Wires Private Limited was converted into Plaza General Cable Wire Private Limited.

2009
New Brand: PCG

1. PCG brand established under Plaza Power — a sister concern of Plaza Wires — to venture into marketing and sales of PVC conduit pipes, allied accessories and insulated tape.

2. Ventured into the marketing and sales of Aluminium Cables under our home brand "Plaaza Cables".

3. Plaza General Cable Private Limited got converted into Plaza Wires Private Limited.

2010

1. Started manufacturing of multicore cables, submersible cables and other specialised cables in Baddi.

2. Ventured into the marketing and sales of PVC insulated tapes under PCG.

2013

Ventured into marketing and sale of PVC conduit pipes and accessories under our home brand "PCG".

2016
Expansion

Company expanded its operations through establishment of Plaza South India office operations — starting with Kerala and moving to Tamil Nadu and Karnataka.

2019

Launched 3D wires with triple layer bunching and PVC coating.

2020

Ventured into the marketing and sales of ceiling fans, ventilation fans, exhaust fans under "Plaza Cables" brand.

2021
New Brand: Action Wires

1. Launched the brand "Action Wires" to provide quality economical wiring solutions and cater to a wider consumer base.

2. Launched Miniature Circuit Breakers (MCBs) and distribution boards.

2023
IPO Listed

Plaza Wires Limited successfully listed on the stock exchange — a landmark milestone reflecting the confidence and trust of customers and stakeholders across India.
