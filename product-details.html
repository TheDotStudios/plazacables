Product Overview
Flat Submersible Cables (Multicore)

Powering life's most essential resource — water — demands a cable as tough as the environment it operates in. Plaza Flat Submersible Cables are engineered with high-quality copper conductors and durable insulation to deliver reliable, uninterrupted power to submersible pumps in deep wells, agricultural fields and water-management systems. Resistant to moisture, abrasion, grease and oil, they are manufactured under strict quality control to ensure superior mechanical and electrical performance.

Technical Specifications

Standards IS:694, IS:8130, IS:5831, IEC 60245, VDE 0282, BS 6500, AS/NZS 5000, IEC 60227, IEC:332‑1, ASTM‑2863, ASTM‑2843
Range 1.5 sq.mm to 35 sq.mm
Conductor 3‑core flat PVC submersible cables made of electrolytic‑grade, bright annealed bare copper with more than 100% conductivity conforming to IS:8130.
Insulation Bunched conductors insulated with specially formulated high‑grade FR PVC compound giving very high insulation resistance. Cores in Red, Yellow, Blue laid up in flat parallel position.
Outer Sheath Specially formulated PVC compound (type ST‑1), highly resistant to soil and chemicals. Supplied with sequential meter marking.
Marking As per IS:694 specifications with special marking “3 core submersible”.
Testing Tested as per IS:694, IS:8130, IS:5831, IEC 60245, VDE 0282, BS 6500, AS/NZS 5000, IEC 60227, IEC:332‑1, ASTM‑2863, ASTM‑2843.
Packing Supplied in 100 m up to 500 m (±5%) on jute bags & non‑returnable wooden drums. Longer lengths available on request.
Applications
  • Connects underwater submersible pump sets with the main supply line.
  • Suitable for agriculture and irrigation applications.
  • Ideal for domestic installations and other outdoor uses.
  • Specifically designed for submersible pumps in deep wells.
  • Built to perform in physically restrictive and harsh environmental conditions.
  • Manufactured to ensure maximum reliability in demanding installations.
Key Features
  • Three‑core parallel design for heavy‑duty use.
  • Protection against water and small solid particles.
  • Tough black PVC sheath for rough conditions.
  • High moisture and abrasion resistance.
  • Low power consumption and lead‑free PVC.
  • RoHS & REACH compliant with rodent protection.
