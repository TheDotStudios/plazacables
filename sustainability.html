Our Practices

Fifteen pillars of sustainability guiding how we source, manufacture, and deliver every product.

Lead-Free PVC

All PVC used in our products is completely free of lead, ensuring safety for consumers and minimizing environmental impact.

Eco-Friendly Packaging

We minimize plastic in packaging, opting for cardboard and steel drums that can be efficiently reprocessed.

Energy-Efficient Manufacturing

Our facilities use energy-efficient machinery and optimized processes to reduce power consumption across all plants.

Water Conservation

We implement measures to minimize water usage and recycle water throughout our manufacturing operations.

Packaging in which raw materials arrive at our Sikanderabad and Baddi facilities is actively reused, reducing waste at the source.

All chemical drums supplied with raw materials are carefully collected and recycled to support a circular economy.

Wherever possible, raw materials are sourced from certified, environmentally responsible suppliers committed to sustainable practices.

Production waste is minimized through process optimization, and leftover materials are either reused or responsibly sold back to suppliers for recycling.

Regular training for employees on sustainable practices and responsible handling of materials to embed a culture of environmental awareness.

We continually evaluate and upgrade our sourcing and production practices to meet evolving environmental standards and regulations.

Implementing systems to track raw material usage to optimize efficiency and reduce waste across all manufacturing stages.

Not just recycling packaging, but also repurposing scrap materials into new products wherever feasible to close the production loop.

Local Sourcing Wherever Possible

Reducing transportation emissions and supporting local communities by sourcing raw materials near our manufacturing facilities in Sikanderabad and Baddi.
