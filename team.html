In Memoriam
Chairman Emeritus

Late Shri Bhim Sain Gupta

It is with deep respect and gratitude that we remember our Chairman Emeritus, whose vision and values laid the foundation of Plaza Cables' remarkable 50-year journey. A man of grit, foresight and unwavering commitment, he steered Plaza through decades of growth, challenges and transformation — making quality not just a benchmark, but the very cornerstone of the organisation.

Though he is no longer with us, his legacy lives on in every milestone we achieve, every product we deliver and every relationship we build. The strength of our past, built under his stewardship, makes the journey ahead even more inspiring.

“Quality is not an act, but a habit. If we remain committed to excellence and stay true to our values, no challenge is too great, and no dream too distant.”
