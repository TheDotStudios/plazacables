Corporate Policies

QHSE & Governance Policy.

Plaza Cables Group is committed to excellence through an integrated Quality, Health, Safety, and Environment management system. Every employee shares responsibility for upholding these standards.

01

Product Excellence

Deliver high-quality products on time to achieve full customer satisfaction.

02

Employee Engagement

Encourage all employees to actively participate in QHSE initiatives.

03

Health & Safety

Provide a safe and healthy workplace by identifying and mitigating hazards across operations.

04

Environmental Responsibility

Prevent pollution and use resources efficiently across every stage of operations.

05

Continuous Improvement

Regularly review and enhance the effectiveness of the QHSE management system.

06

Resource Allocation

Ensure sufficient resources are committed to maintain and improve QHSE standards.

07

Transparency

Conduct business openly, except where security or business interests require discretion.

08

Philosophy

Uphold transparency, accountability and propriety in all interactions, enhancing long-term shareholder value.

09

Regulatory Compliance

Strictly follow all legal and regulatory requirements; legal-sensitive decisions are documented with expert opinions.
